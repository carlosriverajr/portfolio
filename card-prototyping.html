AI Prototyping Examples

Group by Deal

Clients needed a better way to view related financial instruments. I created a prototype that grouped individual debt instruments by deal. User testing validated the approach with users reporting improved comprehension and workflow efficiency.

Flexed View

Clients needed a clearer way to understand how bond and loan terms change over time. I built a prototype that highlighted key “before and after” values, making changes easy to track and compare. User testing confirmed the approach improved clarity and made workflows more efficient by saving time.
